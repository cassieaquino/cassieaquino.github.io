    Design and psychology are linked to one another and can be understood through the Gestalt principles. The principles are built on the idea of emergence, reification, multi-stability, and invariance. These four principles show how the brain fills in gaps and to help us understand things that we see. Emergence is the recognition of elements through outlines, reification is the matching of familiar patterns with elements that have missing parts, multi-stability is the interpretation of ambiguous elements, and invarience is recognizing simple objects independent of their composition.
